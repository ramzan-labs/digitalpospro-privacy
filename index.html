DigitalPosPro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

1. Introduction

DigitalPosPro is a desktop Point of Sale (POS) application designed for retail businesses to manage sales, inventory, customers, suppliers, accounting (Khata), expenses, and comprehensive business operations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the DigitalPosPro desktop application.

By downloading and using DigitalPosPro, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address - Used as your username for account authentication
  • Password - Stored securely using encryption for authentication
  • Name and contact details - Business name, phone number (optional)
  • Organization details - Business type, organization ID

2.2 Business Data

  • Sales transactions - Including product details, quantities, prices, dates, payment methods
  • Returns and refunds - Sales returns, purchase returns, refund records
  • Customer information - Names, contact details, purchase history, account balances (Khata)
  • Supplier information - Supplier names, contact details, purchase records, payment history
  • Inventory data - Product names, descriptions, stock levels, pricing, variants, barcodes
  • Purchase records - Purchase orders, supplier invoices, stock additions
  • Expense records - Business expenses, categories, payment details
  • Accounting data (Khata) - Ledger entries, customer/supplier accounts, credit/debit balances
  • Employee data - Staff names, roles, permissions, activity logs
  • Scanning data - Barcode/QR scan history, product lookup records

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device information - Operating system version, app version, device fingerprint for security
  • Usage data - App performance metrics, error logs for troubleshooting, feature usage statistics
  • Authentication tokens - Secure tokens for API access and session management
  • Activity logs - User actions, screen access, changes to business data
  • AI assistant interactions - Chat history, AI usage metrics (if AI features are enabled)

2.4 Optional Data

  • Product images - If you upload product photos
  • Receipt images - If you use bill scanning features
  • Profile images - User avatars (optional)
  • Business logo - For receipt customization and branding
  • Theme preferences - Light/dark mode selection
  • Language preferences - Selected language for the interface

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core functionality - Process sales, manage inventory, track customers, handle returns, manage purchases
  • Accounting and ledger management - Maintain Khata accounts, track balances, generate reports
  • Authenticate your account - Verify your identity and secure access with role-based permissions
  • Sync data across devices - Enable cloud synchronization for multi-device and multi-location access
  • AI assistance - Provide intelligent product matching, customer insights, and sales assistance (if enabled)
  • Improve the application - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Provide customer support - Troubleshoot issues and assist with technical problems
  • Ensure security - Detect and prevent fraudulent activity using device fingerprinting
  • Generate reports - Create sales, inventory, and business analytics reports
  • Customize receipts - Apply your branding to printed receipts
  • Comply with legal obligations - Meet regulatory requirements

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

DigitalPosPro stores a copy of your business data locally on your device using SQLite database for offline functionality. This data remains on your device and is encrypted where applicable. In Electron/desktop mode, all data is stored locally in your user data folder, with optional cloud synchronization.

4.2 Cloud Storage

Your data is synchronized with our cloud servers to enable:

  • Multi-device access across desktop and web platforms
  • Data backup and recovery
  • Real-time synchronization across your business locations
  • AI assistant features and cloud-based analytics
  • Remote management and monitoring

Cloud synchronization can be disabled for fully offline operation.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption - Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication - Password hashing and token-based authentication
  • Access controls - Role-based permissions for different user types
  • Regular security updates - Patches and security improvements
  • Secure servers - Cloud infrastructure with security certifications

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our application:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment processing services (if integrated)
  • AI/ML services for intelligent features (anonymized and aggregated data where possible)
  • Analytics services (aggregated, anonymized data only)
  • Email delivery services for notifications

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To prevent fraud or security threats
  • In response to court orders or government requests

5.3 Business Transfers

If we sell or transfer our business, your data may be transferred to the new owner.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Maintain business records
  • Comply with legal obligations

You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct - Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete - Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export - Download your business data in a portable format
  • Opt-out - Disable cloud synchronization and use offline mode only
  • Withdraw consent - Revoke permissions for data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the application
  • Sending an email notification (if you have provided an email address)
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app

Your continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Offline Mode

DigitalPosPro offers full offline functionality through Electron/desktop mode. When operating offline:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • Synchronization occurs when you reconnect to the internet (if cloud sync is enabled)
  • You retain full control over your offline data
  • All core features remain available including sales, inventory, and reporting

12. Device Fingerprinting

For security purposes, DigitalPosPro uses device fingerprinting to:

  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Identify suspicious login attempts
  • Protect your business data from fraud
  • Enable security center features

Device fingerprints are generated using browser/device-specific identifiers and are stored locally and securely on our servers for security verification only.

  • AI/ML services - For intelligent product matching, customer insights, and sales assistance
  • Analytics - For app performance monitoring (anonymized data only)
  • Cloud storage - For data backup and synchronization
  • Email services - For sending notifications and reports

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review these policies.
