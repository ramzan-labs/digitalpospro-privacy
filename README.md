# DigitalPosPro Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction

DigitalPosPro is a desktop Point of Sale (POS) application designed for retail businesses to manage sales, inventory, customers, suppliers, accounting (Khata), expenses, and comprehensive business operations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the DigitalPosPro desktop application.

By downloading and using DigitalPosPro, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Password
- Name and contact details
- Organization details

### Business Data
- Sales transactions
- Customer and supplier information
- Inventory data
- Purchase records
- Expense records
- Accounting data (Khata)
- Employee data
- Scanning data

### Technical Data
- Device information
- Usage data
- Authentication tokens
- Activity logs
- AI assistant interactions

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide core functionality, authenticate your account, enable synchronization, improve the application, provide support, ensure security, generate reports, customize receipts, and comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

DigitalPosPro stores business data locally on your device using SQLite for offline usage, and may synchronize data with cloud servers when enabled. We use encryption, secure authentication, access controls, and regular security updates to protect your data.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers, legal authorities, or in the event of a business transfer when necessary and permitted by law.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as needed to provide services, maintain business records, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

## 7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, export, opt-out of cloud synchronization, or withdrawal of consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@smartpospro.me.
